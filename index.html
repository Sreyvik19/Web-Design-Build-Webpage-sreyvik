Sports

The activity involving physical exertion and skill in which an individual or team competes against another or others for entertainment.

Football

The objective is to score by getting the ball into the opposing team's goal.

Volleyball

The team sport where two teams of six players each compete on a rectangular court divided by a net.

Basketball

The team sport played between two teams of five players on a rectangular court.

Tennis

The sport played with rackets and a ball, where two players (singles) or two teams of two players (doubles) hit a ball back and forth over a net on a rectangular court.

Rocket

The group of dynamic and exciting sports that are hugely popular around the world

Petanque game

The target sport, where participants throw metal balls as close as possible to a small wooden ball called a cochonnet or jack while standing inside a circle with both feet on the ground.

Aerobic

The type of physical activity that increases your heart rate and breathing for a sustained period, helping improve your heart, lungs, and overall fitness.

Walking, running, cycling, swimming, dancing, Zumba, jumping rope. Goal: Improves cardiovascular health, burns calories, and boosts endurance. "Aerobic" means: "With oxygen" – your body uses oxygen to generate energy during the activity.
